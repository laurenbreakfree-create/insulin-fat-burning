🥗 “But I eat healthy — and barely anything!”

This is the maze almost everyone gets lost in. “Healthy” and “low-cal” aren’t the same as what your body needs right now. A clean meal can still spike insulin, and eating too little spikes stress — which pushes insulin right back up. So this lever isn’t about eating less or cleaner. It’s about eating the way that keeps your insulin steady.
